Yachats Dance Jam Weekend

Come Dance with us at the beautiful, seaside town of Yachats, on the Oregon Coast. Three(3) nights of Ecstatic Dance, with daytime sessions of Contact Improv, Singing, Breath work, and more. Walk on the beach, explore the little beach town of Yachats, or the hike in the nearby Cape Perpetua National Preserve. The Yachats Inn has a lovely dance space and kitchens available for our use over the weekend.

Reservations for the Yachats Dance Weekend January 13th-16th, 2023
This reservation is for the Dance Jam Event only, and is your donation for paying the DJ's and hall/other expenses. This event is run and kept going by volunteers, working to keep it low cost for everyone. If you intend to book a room at the Yachats Inn for the weekend, please call the Yachats Inn directly at (541) 547-3456 and ask for the Dance Weekend rate. The donation below is for the dance event only, it does not cover your lodging at the Inn.

Staying at the Yachats Inn

The Yachats Inn suites can sleep four(4) people comfortably, with a queen bed, a foldout couch, a kitchenette with refrigerator, stove, coffee machine, pans, and service for four(4). Please make your Room reservations by calling the Yachats Inn directly at (541) 547-3456 and asking for the Dance Weekend rate. The Dance Weekend rate is the best deal, as we get three(3) nights for the price of two (2)! Check in after 3pm on Friday, and Checkout by 11am on Monday. (Do not use the Yachats Inn Website for reservations, you won"t get the special weekend rate.) We have a limited number of spaces available for RV parking, with the Event hall bathrooms and kitchen available for use. If you plan on RV camping (sorry no tent camping), please let us know in advance. Send mail to "dancejam@patternagents.com" with any questions.
