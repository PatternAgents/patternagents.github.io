JTAG 20 Pin Female to 10 Pin Male Cable Adapter

The JTAG 20 Pin Female to 10 Pin Male Cable Adapter (JTAG20F-JTAG10M) is designed to enable the user to connect 10 pin programmer headers to development boards that require a standard 20 Pin JTAG connection.

JTAG20F-JTAG10M Features:

  • JTAG20F-JTAG10M - JTAG 20 Pin Female to 10 Pin Male Cable Adapter.
