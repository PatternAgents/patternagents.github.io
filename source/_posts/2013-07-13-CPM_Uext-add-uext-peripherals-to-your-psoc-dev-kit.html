The CPM Uext Adaptor board is designed to enable Cypress PSoC development boards to utilize the dozens of Uext Peripherals boards that are available today.

CPM_Uext Adaptor Board Features :

  • Cypress CY8CKIT-001 - Supports up to three (3) CPM_Uext
  • Cypress CY8CKIT-030 - Supports up to two (2) CPM_Uext
  • Cypress CY8CKIT-050 - Supports up to two (2) CPM_Uext
  • Cypress CY3280-28xxx - Supports one (1) CPM_Uext
  • Cypress CY3280-22x45 - Supports one (1) CPM_Uext
  • Selectable 3.3Volt or 5.0Volt Operation

(Not an Exhaustive list as more Cypress development boards are routinely released by Cypress Semiconductor.)

For more information on Uext peripherals, see Uext on WikiPedia.
