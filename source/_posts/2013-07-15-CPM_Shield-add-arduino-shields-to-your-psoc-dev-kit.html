The CPM Shield Adaptor board is designed to enable Cypress PSoC development boards to utilize the hundreds of Arduino shield boards that are available today.

{{page.product_name}} Features:

  • Cypress CY8CKIT-001 - Supports up to three (3) CPM_Shield
  • Cypress CY8CKIT-030 - Supports up to two (2) CPM_Shield
  • Cypress CY8CKIT-050 - Supports up to two (2) CPM_Shield
  • Cypress CY3280-28xxx - Supports one (1) CPM_Shield
  • Cypress CY3280-22x45 - Supports one (1) CPM_Shield
  • Selectable 3.3Volt or 5.0Volt Operation

(Not an Exhaustive list as more Cypress development boards are routinely released by Cypress Semiconductor.)

For more information on Arduino, see www.arduino.cc.
