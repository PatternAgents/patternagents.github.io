The JTAG Mini-Breakout for STM32 Discovery (JTAG10M-STLV2F) is designed to enable the STM32 Discovery boards to be used as a general purpose JTAG programming tool.

{{page.product_name}} Features:

  • JTAG10M-STLV2F - Adapts ST/Link V2 pinout to standard 10 pin JTAG pinout.

For more information on STM32 Discovery boards, see STM32 Discovery.
