The StickSoC Arduino Shield Adaptor for PSoC4 allows the Cypress CY8CKIT-049 to accept Arduino Shields.

{{page.product_slug}} Features:

  • DC Barrel Jack for Providing VIN, 12Vdc-16Vdc to Shields that need it
  • PTC Resetable Fuse for VIN
  • USB/5.0Vdc to 3.3Volt Voltage Regulator
  • Arduino Uno/Leonardo Compatible Shield

About {{page.product_slug}}:

"StickSoC" is a very quick (two hour) hack to extend the capability of the CY8CKIT-049 PSoC4 "Gumstick" Development board.

{{page.product_slug}} Application Examples:

  • Use Arduino Shields on PSoC4
  • Add LCD Display
  • Add Interfaces
