What do Smart Objects have to do with Device Trees?

It seems like the low level hardware platform folks aren't talking to the high level "object" definition folks, and there are more than a few parallel efforts underway there. It makes it all rather challenging for us simple makers, that just want to utilize these platforms to get something done. I'd like to see a more coordinated approach to a common Device Tree Object directory/definition, and I think that the Internet Protocol Smart Objects (ISPO) "starter pack" is a great template for how to do that.
