The AB_ESP8266 WiFi interface for Xbee Sockets allows the ESP8266 to be used with Arduino FIO and other XBee based platforms.

{{page.product_slug}} Features:

  • ESP8266 WiFi SoC
  • XBee Socket Form Factor
  • Jumper straps for reprogramming
  • LEDs for status indictors

About {{page.product_slug}}:

"AB_ESP8266" is a very quick hack to put the popular ESP8266 WiFI interface on the XBee form factor.

{{page.product_slug}} Application Examples:

  • WiFi Sensor
  • WiFi Bridge
