Agent-MIDI-IN-FeatherWing is an Adafruit Feather compatible board with a MIDI output driver and 3.5mm TRS jack interface.

Agent-MIDI-IN-FeatherWing - MIDI Output Board :

The Agent-MIDI-IN-FeatherWing is a low cost, embeddable module for MIDI output :

  • MIDI Output Driver
  • 3.5mm TRS jack interface
  • Solder Jumper Pin Selection - Capable of using four(4) together
  • Power Indicator LED
  • Adafruit Feather Compatible form factor
